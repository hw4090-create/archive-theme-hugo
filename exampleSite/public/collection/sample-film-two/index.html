A short newsreel produced in 1976 — typical of the newsreel-style shorts distributed to rural projection teams during this period.

Provenance

Donated by a former rural projectionist, 2019.

Interpretive note

Brief newsreels like this one were designed to lead into longer programs at mobile projection events. They circulated quickly and were often retained on the reel shelf for reuse across multiple screenings.
