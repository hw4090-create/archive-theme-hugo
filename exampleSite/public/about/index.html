About

What this archive is and why it exists.

This archive is a small, curated public exhibition rather than a bulk repository.

Its focus is on making rare or overlooked film formats legible to a general audience — through historical context, material detail, and a small set of representative surviving prints.

Scope

The project treats film as an object, a technology, and a record of circulation. It asks how a given format carried education, entertainment, or ideology, and shows how rare prints can be digitized today with care.

How this site is built

This site is built with Hugo and the open-source archive-theme. All content is written in plain Markdown and can be edited by anyone familiar with text files.
