Welcome. This site is a demonstration of the **archive-theme** — a bilingual Hugo theme for film and video archive exhibitions.

It supports locally-hosted video playback, structured metadata for each entry, centered images and galleries that won't break your layout, and clean, content-first pages driven entirely by Markdown.

Use the menu above to explore the sections, or open the **Collection** to see how individual archive items are presented.
